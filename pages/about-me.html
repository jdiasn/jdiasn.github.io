About me

Hi, I'm physicist. Since my firsts years in physics undergrad I became interested in environmental physical processes. I'm currently working to evaluate Aerosol Optical Thickness retrieved by the VIIRS sensor.

I realized that actually doing physics is much more enjoyable than just learning it. Maybe 'doing it' is the right way of learning. (Gerd Binnig - Nobel Prize 1989)
