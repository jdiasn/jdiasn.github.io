AERONET in South America

Sa 30 April 2016

At the present time there are nine AERONET station in operation across Brazil. Five from these stations are placed in the Amazon Region and the four others are spread out over the remaining territory. The AOT measured data may be classified as level 1.5 and 2.0. This classification is associated with the algorithm applied during the data processing.
